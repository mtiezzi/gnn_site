Matteo Tiezzi, Giuseppe Marra, Stefano Melacci, Marco Maggini and Marco Gori (2020). "A Lagrangian Approach to Information Propagation in Graph Neural Networks; ECAI2020

Bibtex:

@article{tiezzi2020lagrangian,
